Lango

March.2014 - July.2014


Lango is interactive cubes specially desiged for language learning.

Each cube diplays a random letter. If you make a word with three different cubes, they show a corresponding picture and words in different languages like Korean or Chinese.

Lango from Saebom Kwon on Vimeo.

Tool : Arduino, processing

Co-creator: Saebom Kwon and Jaejoong Kim.

Remembar

March.2014 - July.2014


Remembar is a new type of digital clock specially designed for daily scheduling. It can interact with post-its(paper memo) on which you write down your daily plans or memo. In this project, we focused on the fact that people still enjoy using analogue scheduling method like post-its even though there are numerous number of mobile scheduling apps or planners. We considered new method to combine analogue and digital experience.

You can scribble anything on a paper memo and just attach it to a specific time section of Remembar. If there's any memo on a certain time section, Remembar send you a notice via your phone at the time.

Remembar from SeYeong Kim on Vimeo.

Tool : Arduino, processing

Co-creator: Saebom Kwon

2014 Physical Computing class work, Art & Technology, Sogang University.

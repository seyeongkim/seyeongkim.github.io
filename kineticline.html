KineticLine

Fall.2017

Unlike many other works that deliver information using lines (e.g. graphs...), we present KineticLine that transfers vibration and liveliness into media art form through the physical line interface. KineticLine consists of sixteen servo motors placed at regular intervals, which dynamically formulate diverse line shapes.

Fall .2017, Media Interaction Design Course, Department of Industrial Design, KAIST
